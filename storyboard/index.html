One Must Always Prove Their Bridges

A light inherits,
blushes, and it does not return -
the enchanting color of this crystal planetarium.
Like how the somber brick of communities that water the domestic forests
are trusted.
I get the feeling
they must have lots to imbue
to each other since
the value imposes necessity
and necessity equates love.
